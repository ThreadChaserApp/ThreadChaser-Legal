2. Description of the App

ThreadChaser is an iOS application that assists users in identifying and matching mechanical thread standards by comparing measured thread dimensions against a built-in reference database. The App supports a range of international and national thread standards including, but not limited to, Metric (ISO), UNC/UNF/UNEF (ASME), BSP/BSW/BSF/BA (BS), NPT (ASME), ACME, Buttress, Square, and Knuckle (DIN) threads.

The App is intended to serve as a reference and identification aid only. It is not a substitute for professional engineering judgment, formal metrology equipment, or certified inspection processes.
